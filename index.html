car/gar/zar Verbs

Input the verb to be conjugated in the preterite Yo form, should be a -car, -gar, or -zar verb
This does assume they're otherwise regular and doesn't allow 2-letter verbs

Explanation

-car, -gar, and -zar verbs change their yo-form conjugation in the preterite tense, to preserve pronunciation. car: -qué gar: -gué zar: -cé
